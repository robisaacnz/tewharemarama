Te Whare Marama provides computers and Internet access to residents and staff. This service is provided for free, but has some rules that must be followed.

Don't use the service for anything illegal. This includes downloading pirated music or movies.

Don't use the service to threaten, abuse or harass anyone.

Don't use the service to tell anyone your location or the location of the refuge.

Don't use the service to talk about other residents, even in private conversations.

Don't upload photos that show the refuge, where the refuge is, or anyone staying at the refuge.

If you break any of these rules, we may turn off your access to the service.
